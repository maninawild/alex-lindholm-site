Most people are using AI like a chatbot.
That’s about 1% of its real value.
The real leverage appears when AI becomes a thinking partner for your business – helping you analyze, plan, and execute faster than a traditional team could.
Here are 10 prompts founders can use to unlock the other 99% when raising capital and scaling revenue:
1. Instant Capital Assessment
Ask AI to analyze your metrics and estimate how much non-dilutive funding or venture capital your company could realistically raise today.
2. Product-Led Growth Playbook
Turn GPT into a PLG advisor using frameworks inspired by Wes Bush – mapping onboarding, activation, and expansion loops.
3. Financial Statement Analyst
Upload your financials and have AI act like a CFO: highlight risks, burn rate trends, and margin opportunities.
4. Tax Strategy Assistant
Ask AI to review your structure and suggest legal ways to optimize taxes, deductions, and cash flow. Then talk to the people.
5. Podcast Launch Strategy
Build a full growth plan for launching a podcast – including distribution, guest strategy, and audience flywheels. Not suitable for all the products though.
6. Strategy Report Generator
Use consulting-style frameworks to generate structured strategy documents similar to those produced by top consulting firms.
7. Founder-Led LinkedIn Pipeline
Design a content system that attracts enterprise buyers and converts attention into qualified conversations.
8. 3-Year Growth Roadmap
Ask AI to draft a structured multi-year strategy covering product, market expansion, and revenue milestones.
9. Meeting Generation System
Generate outbound messaging strategies that convert into meetings and pipeline growth.
10. Cap Table Analysis
Upload your cap table and ask AI to evaluate dilution risk, investor incentives, and long-term founder control.
The real shift with AI isn’t faster answers.
It’s better thinking systems.
Founders who learn to structure their prompts like operators – not casual users – will get dramatically more leverage from the same tools.
