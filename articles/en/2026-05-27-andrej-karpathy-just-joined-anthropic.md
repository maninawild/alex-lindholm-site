Big signal from the AI world today 👀
Andrej Karpathy just joined Anthropic.
For context:
– founding member at OpenAI
– former Tesla AI lead
– one of the most respected people in modern AI
And after leaving OpenAI, he could’ve done almost anything:
start a company, raise a fund, build independently.
Instead, he chose Anthropic.
That says a lot.
Because the AI race is slowly shifting from:
“who has the coolest chatbot?” to:
“who builds the best environment for the next generation of builders?”
And this may be the biggest shift people still underestimate.
One person with the right tools can suddenly operate like a small startup.
Not theoretically.
Operationally.
Code.
Design.
Research.
Agents.
Deployment.
That changes startup economics completely.
Karpathy also mentioned the next few years in LLM development will be “especially formative.”
Translation: we are still absurdly early.
And the interesting part is what top researchers now seem to care about:
– reasoning
– agents
– infrastructure
– long-term architecture
– real-world workflows
Not just bigger chatbots.
Funny thing – most people still think AI is about replacing jobs.
Meanwhile, the deeper shift is leverage.
The gap between:
“person with idea”
and
“person capable of executing the idea”
is collapsing very fast.
And honestly?
Most institutions are not psychologically ready for what that means yet.
If you are capable to execute person - talk to us. We will help with the resources.
