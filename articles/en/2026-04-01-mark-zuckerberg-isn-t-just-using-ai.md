Mark Zuckerberg isn’t just using AI.
He’s building an AI agent to help run Meta 😳
And it’s already happening.
What it does:
– pulls data across a 78,000-person org
– skips management layers
– surfaces real issues before meetings
– tracks what was said vs what got done
Less reporting.
More reality.
Inside Meta, employees are building their own agents:
“My Claw” – connects docs, chats, knowledge
“Second Brain” – AI chief of staff
Some results already:
– engineering output up ~30%
– power users up 80% YoY
That’s not marginal.
That’s structural.
Zuckerberg is talking about “flattening teams” and “elevating individual contributors”.
Translation:
less layers, less information distortion.
And maybe the biggest shift:
Companies won’t just have org charts.
They’ll have agent layers running in parallel.
