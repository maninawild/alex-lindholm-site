Top 10 things founders say… and what they actually mean 👇
“We built proprietary AI”
→ we’re calling an API
“We grew 200% last month”
→ from 3 users to 9
“Our business model is disruptive”
→ we haven’t figured out how to make money yet
“We’re going global”
→ one signup from another country
“We’re in stealth mode”
→ there’s nothing to show yet
“We have a world-class team”
→ a couple of students helping part-time
“Our unit economics are strong”
→ if everything goes perfectly in 5–10 years
“We’re getting strong inbound”
→ someone liked a post
“We’ve built a great culture”
→ we sometimes hang out after work
“We’ve reached product–market fit”
→ a few people didn’t churn immediately
The funny part?
Every founder says at least a few of these at some point.
The real shift happens when you stop optimizing for how things sound…
and start focusing on what’s actually true.
Because in startups:
Reality always shows up eventually.
